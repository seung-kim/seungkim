About Me

I am a Senior Research Scientist at NVIDIA. Starting Fall 2026, I will join KAIST AI as an Assistant Professor. I will be recruiting students for my new lab, with more details to follow soon.


I obtained my PhD at the University of Toronto, advised by Prof. Sanja Fidler. Before that, I earned my MSc and BSc degrees from the same university.


My research interests focus on generative models and their applications in robotic applications and physical AI. I am driven by the challenge of building world models that can simulate and understand complex environments.
